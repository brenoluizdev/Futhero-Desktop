# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors, maintainers, and members of the FUTHERO Desktop community pledge to create a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment for everyone.  
We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for all.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive  
- Being open to new ideas  
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback  
- Taking responsibility for mistakes  

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks  
- Discriminatory comments  
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information  
- Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting  

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and may:
- Remove or edit comments, commits, code, or issues that violate this Code of Conduct  
- Ban or restrict contributors who engage in unacceptable behavior  

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within all project spaces  
- In public or private interactions related to the project  

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via:
- Email: **futhero.contact@gmail.com**  
- Discord DM to a project maintainer  

All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Warning
A private and clear communication identifying the violation.

### 2. Temporary Restriction
A temporary ban or restriction from project spaces.

### 3. Permanent Ban
Applied to severe or repeated violations.
